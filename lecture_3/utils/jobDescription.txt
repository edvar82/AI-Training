Job Title: Full Stack Developer

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and maintaining both front-end and back-end components of our web applications. This role requires expertise in multiple programming languages and frameworks, with a strong understanding of how all parts of the web development stack work together.

Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and maintain both front-end and back-end web applications
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features
- Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to team best practices

Required Skills:
- 3+ years of experience in full stack development
- Proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3
- Experience with front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js
- Experience with back-end languages such as Node.js, Python, or Java
- Knowledge of server-side frameworks like Express, Django, or Spring
- Familiarity with database technologies (SQL: MySQL, PostgreSQL; NoSQL: MongoDB)
- Experience with RESTful APIs and microservices architecture
- Version control with Git
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Understanding of Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Experience with test-driven development
- Knowledge of security and data protection best practices

Education:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent practical experience)
